Shoe History

Shoes date back over 7,000 years, evolving from simple leather coverings to advanced engineered footwear. Ancient Egyptians used sandals made from papyrus, while modern sneakers incorporate cutting-edge materials for performance and comfort.

Interesting Facts

The oldest known shoe is over 5,500 years old.
High heels were originally worn by men in the 10th century.
Sneakers got their name because they are quiet.
The global footwear industry is worth billions.
